Overview

Big X The Plug, also known as Xavier Landum, is an up-and-coming star on the hip-hop scene. Born in Dallas, TX, he gained recognition through exciting and energized performances and catchy tracks. Two of his most notable tracks are 'Texas' and 'Mmhmm,' which blend southern hip-hop and trap. He often talks about his experiences growing up in the city, and his growth to fame. Notable Songs

Big X the Plug's music is known for its hard-hitting lyrics and trap beats. Some of his notable songs include "The Largest," "Texas," and "Mmhmm." These tracks showcase his signature style, which often includes witty verses about his wealth, success, and street credibility. His music frequently features a blend of Southern rap and modern trap influences and is delivered with aggressive flows and hard-hitting lyrics. "The Largest" is a braggy anthem about himself and his unique style, while "Texas" is a regional song paying homage to his home state. Other songs like "Mmhmm" portray his style and unique deep voice.
