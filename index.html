Overview

Big X The Plug, also known as Xavier Landum, is an up-and-coming star on the hip-hop scene. Born in Dallas, TX, he gained recognition through exciting and energized performances and catchy tracks. Two of his most notable tracks are 'Texas' and 'Mmhmm,' which blend southern hip-hop and trap. He often talks about his experiences growing up in the city, and his growth to fame.
